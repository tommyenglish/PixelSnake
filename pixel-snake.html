█ █████ Pixel Snake █████ █

Your goal is to make your snake as long as possible by eating fruit and bugs while avoiding collisions with the walls and the snake itself.

Click in the arena and use the arrow keys (up, down, left, right) to control the snake's direction.

Each fruit and bug eaten will make your snake longer. Every fourth fruit eaten adds a bug to the arena.

Can you get to 40? For an additional challenge, can you avoid bugs and have 6 or more on the board at the same time?
